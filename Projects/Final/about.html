Welcome to Daniel Craun's homepage!

Daniel is a digital artist from Shelby Township, Michigan. He is currently majoring in Media Arts and Technology at Michigan State University. He is a senior specializing in video game design with a great passion for games, animation, and digital art.

Daniel can be contacted through Facebook or by email at craundan@msu.edu

About Daniel

Daniel grew up in Shelby Township, Michigan and has always had a passion for creating things. In his spare time, he enjoys playing making art, playing video games, and playing card and board games. One day he hopes to become a successful artist or designer for video games, animation, or other kinds of media.
